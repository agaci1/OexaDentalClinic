# User Stories

1. As a patient, I want to create an account so that I can use the system.
2. As a patient, I want to book an appointment so that I can visit a dentist.
4. As a patient I want to book a pre-Appointment as children need special treatment.
5. As a patient I get emails and reminders depending on the steps I take on the system.
6. As a patient I can check my previeous appoinments on the clinic.
7. As a patient I get promotions and discounts or not based on my history.
8. As a patient, I want to cancel an appointment if I cannot attend.
9. As a patient, I want to rate the dentist after the appointment.
10. As a patient, I want to receive email notifications when my appointment status changes.
11. As a dentist, I want to see my scheduled appointments so that I know which patients I will treat.
12. As a dentist, I want to mark an appointment as completed.
13. As a dentist I can view patients records and history.
14. As a dentist I can update treatments and diagnosis.
15. As a manager, I want to manage dentists so that the clinic staff is organized.
16. As a manager I can manage the entire system, create and controll users.
17. As a manager I take care of the customer-dentist relationshop flow by doing the job as a recepsionist.
18. As a marketer I manage the visuals and marketing part of the web.
